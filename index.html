    <h1> What is this website? </h1>

    This website delves into the question “What makes code mortal?”, by exploring three 
 key areas social, legal, and ethical concerns in coding.
    
    <h2> Research Topics: </h2>

    Social: The social section examines the societal impacts of coding, 
 including issues of social justice and equity.
    Legal: The legal segment explores the regulatory frameworks and laws that 
 influence ethical coding practices.
    Ethical: The ethical area addresses the moral principles guiding coders and 
 the philosophical aspects of coding.
    
    <h3> What is the goal? </h3>

    This website aims to enlighten and engage its audience on the ethical concerns of 
 coding, fostering responsible, socially conscious, and legally compliant practices.
